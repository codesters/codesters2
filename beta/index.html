our aim

We get excited about the technologies being used to develop web apps, mobile apps and other softwares. Our main aim is to share our knowledge and collaborate on different projects which can solve some problems that we have.

who are we?

We are passionate software developers and hackers from India. Some of us are students and always learning new technologies and tinkering with them. Visit our Projects page to take a look at our currrent projects.

techologies we work with

We currently work with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript(also JQuery), Python(django), Wordpress for develpoing websites and mobile & web apps. For desktop softwares we use C, C++ and Python. This coming summer we will be digging JAVA and Obj C for developing mobile apps for Android and iOS respectively. We love Open-Source and look forward to every oppurtunity in helping the Open-Source community.

connect with us

We are always open to suggestions to improve our Group. We will be glad to help you in some project or just for a coffee near Delhi(India). Connect with any of our memeber on facebook, Google+ or twitter. OR you can drop a mail(info at codesters dot org).
