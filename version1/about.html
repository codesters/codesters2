about Us

Codesters is a group initiated by a bunch of tech addicts with a view to provide a common platform for others of our kind to brainstorm on various projects.

We at Codesters believe in sharing knowledge and exploiting minds to its full potential. If you are addicted to coding in a way or just want to keep up with the technology, you are at the right place.

With various events and meetups we allow a better understanding among codesters, and collectively implementing an idea and working on projects enhances our members' skills as a programmer.

connect with us

We are always open to suggestions to improve our Group. We will be glad to help you in some project or just for a coffee near Delhi(India). Connect with any of our memeber on facebook, Google+ or twitter. OR you can drop a mail(info at codesters dot org).
